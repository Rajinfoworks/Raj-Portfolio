Types of Wireframes

Low-Fidelity: Sketch-style, focuses on layout and flow.

Mid-Fidelity: Adds spacing, grid alignment, and some text.

High-Fidelity: More detailed, close to final design but still without visuals.

Our Wireframing Process

1. Research: Gather requirements, define user personas, analyze competitors.
2. User Flows: Map out journeys users will take across the product.
3. Sketching: Quick drafts to visualize screens and interactions.
4. Wireframing: Create structured layouts with hierarchy.
5. Validation: Share with stakeholders & adjust based on feedback.
