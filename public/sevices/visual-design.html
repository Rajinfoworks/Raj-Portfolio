Core Principles of Visual Design

1. Balance: Maintain harmony between visual elements.

2. Contrast: Use colors and typography to highlight key content.

3. Alignment: Ensure structured layouts for clarity.

4. Consistency: Reuse patterns for familiarity and usability.

5. Accessibility: Design for inclusivity and diverse users.
