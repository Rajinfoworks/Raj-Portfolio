🖌️ Prototyping & Wireframes

Prototyping and wireframing bring ideas to life before writing a single line of code. At Rajinfoworks, we help teams move from rough sketches to interactive prototypes — ensuring usability, efficiency, and clarity.

Why It Matters 🎯

  • 📐 Visualize structure and flows early.
  • ⚡ Save development time by testing before coding.
  • 👥 Gather user feedback faster with clickable prototypes.
  • 🎯 Align stakeholders on design direction.

Prototype Stages 🔄

  • ✏️ Low-fidelity wireframes → basic layouts & content blocks.
  • 🎨 Mid-fidelity → UX flows with clearer components.
  • 💻 High-fidelity → pixel-perfect, interactive mockups.
  • 🖱️ Clickable prototypes → user testing & validation.
