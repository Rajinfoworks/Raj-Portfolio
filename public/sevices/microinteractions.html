What are Microinteractions?

Microinteractions are the **tiny, functional animations and responses** that happen within a digital product. From a button ripple to a swipe gesture, they provide instant feedback, make systems easier to use, and bring interfaces to life.

They are the **bridge between user and system**, turning routine tasks into engaging moments.

Core Principles of Microinteractions

1. Trigger: Every microinteraction starts with an action ‚Äî a click, tap, or system event.

2. Feedback: The system responds with visual, audio, or haptic signals.

3. Rules: Define how the interaction behaves under different scenarios.

4. Loops & Modes: Handle repetition, extended states, or special cases gracefully.

Our Microinteraction Design Process

3. Prototype: Design interactions using tools like Figma & After Effects.
4. Implement: Develop lightweight animations with CSS/JS or Lottie.
